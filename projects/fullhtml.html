My Hostel App

Solution for all hostel registers, paperwork and beyond

The challenge

A student has to face a lot of registers during the period of stay at a hostel. The app solves this problem by including all the options that one will need. Also, it could be tough to find people of similar interests, especially if you are new to the place, the app has a section to search people based on their interests and skills. Pals can be called in situations where money can split in a group.
